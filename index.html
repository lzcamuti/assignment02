VACANT STRUCTURES IN YOUNGSTOWN

ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This is a project located in Youngstown, Ohio which seeks to relate the city's numerous vacant residential and industrial properties to biophysical characteristics such as landcover and topography. This map was created in the Scripting Civic Engagement seminar at the University of Virginia (Fall 2017), taught by Andrea Hansen Phillips.

Map data courtesy of Mapbox Studio and the Youngstown State University
